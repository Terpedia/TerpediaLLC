# Team Slide Update Instructions

## Current Status
The team slide has placeholders for LinkedIn information. Please update with actual details.

## Steps to Update

1. **Find LinkedIn Profiles**:
   - Search for Susan's LinkedIn profile
   - Search for Dan's LinkedIn profile
   - Get their full names, titles, and backgrounds

2. **Update the Team Slide**:
   - Replace `[Add from LinkedIn]` with actual background
   - Add previous companies and roles
   - Add education credentials
   - Add key achievements
   - Update LinkedIn URLs

3. **Add Photos**:
   - Save professional photos as:
     - `images/susan-photo.jpg`
     - `images/dan-photo.jpg`
   - Update slide to include photos

4. **Advisory Board**:
   - Identify 3-5 advisors
   - Add their names, titles, and LinkedIn profiles
   - Get permission to list them

## Example Format

```markdown
## Susan [Last Name] - CEO

**Background**: 
- Former [Role] at [Company]
- [Years] years in [Industry]
- [Education] from [University]
- LinkedIn: linkedin.com/in/susan-[username]

**Expertise**:
- Business strategy and market development
- [Specific skill 1]
- [Specific skill 2]
```

## After Updating
- Review the slide for accuracy
- Get approval from team members
- Export new PDF
- Push to GitHub
